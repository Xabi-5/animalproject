This is the platypus

It is a mammal native to the Australian continent, renowned worlwdide by how different it is from any other mammal.

Some of its most unusual characteristics are:

  • It lays eggs
  • Males have venom
  • They posess a beak
The main threats to the survival of this species are:
  1. Deforestation
  2. Pollution of water bodies
  3. Influx of external, non native predators, such as cats
